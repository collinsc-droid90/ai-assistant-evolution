Many designers like myself spend hours, even days, struggling to develop fresh ideas for their projects. My MVP project is called the Inspirer, which is a web-based tool designed to help graphic designers overcome creative blocks and jumpstart their ideation process.

## What does it do? (Or what was it supposed to do?) üôã

When you're staring at a blank canvas and need a creative spark, this app generates unique, structured design prompts by randomly combining three key elements:

- Design Style - An aesthetic direction (e.g., Minimalism, Pop Art, Brutalism)

- Target Audience - A specific demographic to design for (e.g., Gen Z, Small Business Owners, Healthcare Workers)

- Emotion/Concept - A feeling or message to convey (e.g., Nostalgia, Innovation, Confidence)
